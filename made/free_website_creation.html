Part 2: Add a Custom Domain

Step 1: Configure GitHub

  • Go to your repository Settings ‚Üí Pages
  • Under "Custom domain," enter your domain (e.g., ianmoss.com)
  • Click Save and enable "Enforce HTTPS"
