Team Roles

Engineering Product & Market Marketing & Growth User Feedback Design & UI CEO / Presenter

Product Engineering

Example: Ruby on Rails specialist

Responsible for building and evolving the core application. This includes backend development, APIs, testing, performance, and refining AI-generated code so it is production-ready.

Product & Market Strategy

Example: Product manager or market-led founder

Defines the problem being solved, the target users, and the feature priorities. Ensures the product delivers real value rather than just technical novelty.

Marketing & Growth

Example: Marketeer or growth specialist

Focuses on attracting the right users through messaging, PPC, and experiments. Feeds acquisition and conversion insights back into product decisions.

User Feedback & Research

Example: UX researcher or customer advocate

Collects and analyses user feedback, identifies friction points, and ensures real user behaviour shapes future iterations.

Design, UI & Visuals

Example: Product or visual designer

Designs interfaces, user flows, and visual assets. Improves usability, accessibility, and perceived quality across product and marketing.

CEO / Presenter

Example: Founder or spokesperson

Acts as the public face of AwesomeProduct. Communicates vision, presents the product, builds partnerships, and aligns contributors around a shared narrative.
